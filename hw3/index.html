Accodring to "People's Republic of China Labor Law", On the statutory holiday, the employer who works for the laborer pays not less than 300% of the salary.

The daily wage is calculated by dividing the base by the average monthly working days of 21.75 days.

This is how it works: Monthly salary /221.75/8*3*Regular holiday overtime working hours
